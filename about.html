About Me

Ira Consulting and Design

With a career spanning over 26 years in the structural and civil engineering field, Ira brings a unique mix of technical expertise, executive leadership, and operational insight. Ira Consulting and Design was established to bridge the gap between visionary design and practical, high-quality engineering solutions. His background as a producer, director, and marketing strategist, combined with eight years as a business director in the engineering sector, has shaped his ability to not only design and implement plans but also advocate for clients navigating complex city regulations.

Ira has overseen and participated in the production of thousands of projects ranging from complete structural systems to comprehensive civil site design. His institutional knowledge allows him to effectively navigate complex code requirements and city regulations to ensure that our clients receive exceptional service. We strive to be a trusted ally to our clients in their development projects.

Ira's leadership philosophy is simple: create the world you want to live in. When you begin with care, the end result will always be something you can be proud of. On these fundamentals, Ira has built a career of streamlining design processes, mentoring employees and partners, and delivering results that balance integrity, safety, efficiency, and craftsmanship.
