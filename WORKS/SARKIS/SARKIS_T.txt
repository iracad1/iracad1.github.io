The Saint Sarkis Community Center consists of a three-building campus on five acres in Carrollton, Texas. The centerpiece of the campus is a 250-seat church, which is approached through a shaded entry courtyard, positioned between an athletic facility and a community center building. The gently sloping floor of the courtyard brings visitors past a reflecting pool, set beneath an oculus framing a view of the dome of the church beyond. As this complete civil and structural engineering package was designated to become a marquis piece for the city, painstaking care was put into ensuring every detail and feature was executed perfectly to give the community an enduring retreat to gather, celebrate, and worship.
