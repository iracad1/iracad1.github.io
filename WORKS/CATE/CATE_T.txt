The Frisco ISD Career and Technical Education Center is a state-of-the-art, 220,000-square-foot facility dedicated to preparing high school students for the future global workforce. The structure had to be sleek and modern and yet robust enough to serve thousands of students across the district. Reinforced concrete floors founded on piers and a custom steel superstructure with cantilevered classrooms and sprawling open ceilings supported the immaculate architectural design from SHW Group. This thoughtful integration of contemporary materials and warm, wood finishes make the CTE Center a stunning anchor to the existing administration building and high school campus.
